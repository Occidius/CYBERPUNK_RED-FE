As It Unfolds - Pawn Shop Problem: Trend or Targetted Attack?
Author: Rebecca Styx
Date: 11-02-2076
---------------------------------------------
This past week a minor culture rush saw an immense surge of financial and topical street activity in the western sect of Watson bordering the Badlands. A local curio collector and old-world junk hoarder who would only refer to himself as 'Calus the Opulent' recently lodged claims that his shop was the victim of an organized hit that left him entirely devoid of his life stockpile of, in his words: "Bobbles and curios of immeasurable value, some fantastical in nature and others simply irreplaceable as their place in this world is solely singular and untouched by the horrors of the greater, modern world."

When speaking with some of the local shopkeepers and some individuals who were present for the debacle, it was stated that the shop was not targeted but merely subjected to what appears to be a special promotion for the store gone wrong. Many claimed to have witnessed a social media post, of which the original posters have not been found, where an unconfirmed deal was announced that the store was willingly and entirely granting any customer freedom to claim any items they chose entirely for free. Calus claims that he never made such a post, never announced such a promotion, and that the very basic idea of such a thing is "purely and utterly foolish, a complete waste of his own time and effort." The wordy slug then verbally berated our spokesperson and interviewer, after which our team opted to conduct the rest of our investigation without Calus's intervention.

Further investigation into the matter is still pending, but here are the facts confirmed thus far:

1) There were two original social media posts, made just seconds apart from each other, neither of which seem affiliated with the pawn shop

2) According to a few unconfirmed rumors, rising fusion-jazz star Hella Kilatan was seen at the incident, some even claimed she was among the first inside but these rumors as as of yet still unconfirmed

3) The total estimation of everything taken from the store (not including illicit items on the exclusive basement level which the shopkeeper refused to name nor estimate) is set at around 30,000 Eurodollars, however official reports speculate a true price of around 5,000 Eurodollars

4) At present, no official investigation is being launched and NCPD seem reluctant to take the case, citing a lack of pertinence and 'local enthusiasm' to see the case solved



Until further notice, the case seems to have landed in a cold spot. When approached by a new team for further comment it was found that Calus vacated his shop, leaving the location empty and unlocked leading to new squatters being cited in and round the unit. Additionally it was found that Calus is equipped with a portion of an old, out-dated borg frame suffering from chronic overuse, serious neglect, improper or lack of upkeep, and severe corrosion damage. This was confirmed as the bottom half of his borg frame which included modified cyberlegs and rocket boots were found in a custom-designed single-person elevation system linking the upper and lower floors of the pawn shop. At present his whereabouts are unconfirmed but local and city-wide authorities have yet to make comment.

No updates pending, wishing you a safe and savvy weekend, -Rebecca S.
DEVELOPING STORY: - Bloody Scene the Only Aftermath of Concert Confrontation, NC Ghoul Changing Tactis?
Author: Gillean Jordan
Date: 10-25-2076
---------------------------------------------
This past weekend, outside the well-known Japantown Club "*Dark Matter*" a grisly yet shockingly contained scene left behind after what was reported as a cyberpsycho attack has left many Night City occupants wondering about just what the NCPD is actually good for. The scene was originally reported, according to NCPD officers Friday night, by an attendee of an area debut show held that night by up-and-coming Rocker: Hella Kiltan. The performance was Kilatans first in Japantown and is reported to be her biggest event yet, boasting a higher audience retention rate of 45%, new-listener boost of roughly 32%, and a modest recurring audience stat of around 35% (Statistics by Zetatechs 'Aurum-Pop' Engine).

According to a few members of the audience who were interviewed the following day, Kilatan interacted with a heckler she singled out of the crowd. The man, described as white wearing a black suit, red tie, black undershirt, and dress shoes, endured a kick to the head by Kilatan, and as he was escaping the venue was then followed out by a group of three of Kilatans fans, at her direction. No further interactions with the heckler or the three audience members were reported. However, roughly 20 minutes after the end of her set and the closure of Dark Matter for the night, the NCPD responded to a cyberpsycho report in the parking structure behind the club. The corpses of the three audience members were found with a staggering level of brutality and mutilation displayed on their corpses. Including but not limited to dismemberment, bisection, disfiguration and removal of skin, and skeletal rearrangement.

Although the NCPD denied any linked connections, the type and quantity of violence found at the scene matches that of the slaughter we covered on September 28th of this year, and begs the question:

*Has the Ghoul of Night City moved on to terrorizing average, everyday citizens?*

As all ways, we at N54 news will keep you up-to-date.

Developing Story - Self-Proclaimed 'Ghoul of Night City' Strikes Again!
Author: Gillean Jordan
Date: 09-28-2076
---------------------------------------------
On Friday, August 27th in the dead of night, a local vagrant reported discovering 17 dismembered and scattered bodies underneath the northern entrance on-ramp to the West Wind Estate Underwater tunnel.

The bodies were found dismembered, in state of disarray, and appeared to have been there for several days. A note found on the scene, carved into a nearby wall, reads: "The era of the blind and heartless of Night City has come to an end, fear creeps into the homes of the elite, death will find it's way to your heart-" with the rest obscured and destroyed by the NCPD before accurate records could be taken by N54 reporters at the scene. Although they were able to confirm that the message had been signed off by "the Ghoul". The corpses appear to belong to various low-ranking members of Arasaka, Zetatech, and a disproportionate amount of Militech personnel.

The details of the report align with previously collected data on several similar crime scenes found across Night City in the past few months. These include dismembered corpses, unseemingly clean carvings describing grim omens, and not even a speck of biomass tracible to a culprit.

Despite this, the NCPD arrested the homeless man who reported the crime, releasing this statement:

"Rest assured the NCPD is doing everything it can do apprehend the suspect. Do not believe any reports from N54, NC Today, or WNS that claim these events are connected. The brave and valiant members of the NCPD have apprehended any and all culprits related to previous crimes"

This tacks on another strange and seemingly careless move by the NCPD, who continue to arrest a random suspect on sight, and offer no further investigation into these occurrences.
